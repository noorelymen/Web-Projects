02. My work

Interior Design

UHOME Design

UHOME is an E-Commerce platform where a user is able to order their interior design online! After choosing their pack according to the type of space they wish to renovate (from a simple room to an entire home with technical spaces...etc), and submitting a form filled with their space information, they would later (a few days only!) receive a proposition with 3D views, moodboards and a 2D plan to redecorate their home according to their chosen favorite style.
