02. My work

Interior Design

UHOME Design

UHOME is an E-Commerce platform where a user is able to order their interior design online! After choosing their pack according to the type of space they wish to renovate (from a simple room to an entire home with technical spaces...etc), and submitting a form filled with their space information, they would later (a few days only!) receive a proposition with 3D views, moodboards and a 2D plan to redecorate their home according to their chosen favorite style.

Elementor Crocoblock Woocommerce

Sportswear Retail

Retail Brand Experience

Rebrex is a website that introduces the unique concept of REBREX, an algerian corporate specialized in reselling sportswear of different famous global brands . Rebrex has three active stores, each one focusing on a different area: Unibrandz sells sportswear for both men and women, Sneakersouk sells limited edition sneakers and Running 360 sells runnings only.
